💡 Business Tips for Kids

Smart stuff to help your business grow! 🌱

💸

Know Your Costs First

Before you sell anything, add up ALL your costs — paper, ink, staples, everything. Your selling price must be higher than your costs or you'll lose money!

🏷️

Price It Right

A good rule: charge at least 2–3× what it cost to make. If a comic costs $1 to make, charge $2–$3. This is your profit margin!

🖨️

Print Smart

Black-and-white printing is WAY cheaper than color. A laser printer costs about $0.01/page vs $0.10+ for color inkjet. Print a test copy first before making lots!

📣

Tell Everyone!

Word-of-mouth is free advertising. Tell friends, family, and neighbors. Ask happy customers to tell their friends. Make a poster for school (if allowed)!

📓

Write Everything Down

Track every dollar you spend AND make. Use this app! Knowing your numbers helps you make smarter decisions about your business.

🐷

Save Before You Spend

Put at least 20% of profits into savings for your next project. Don't spend all your earnings right away — reinvest some to grow!

🤝

Split Profits Fairly

Working with friends? Decide BEFORE you start how to split the money. Write it down so everyone agrees. Decide who does what work too!

🎁

Make Bundles

Sell Issue 1 + Issue 2 together at a small discount. Bundles feel like a deal for customers and you sell more at once — win-win!

⭐

Quality Matters

A comic that looks great and is fun to read will get people coming back for more. Spend time making something you're proud of!
